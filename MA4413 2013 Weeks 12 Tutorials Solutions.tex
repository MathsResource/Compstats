\section*{Question 1c Solutions}

For source alphabet of size $m$ where each symbol has an equal probability of occurring
\[H(X) = \mbox{log}_2(m)\]
Here there are 8 equally probable symbols.
\[H(X) = \mbox{log}_2(8) = 3\mbox{b/sym}\]
